Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Otto-mated Photography ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our automated Instagram and Facebook publishing application ("Otto-mated").

1. About This Application

Otto-mated is a personal automation tool built and operated solely by Otto Franco for the purpose of automatically publishing nature photography content to his personal Instagram account (@pure_nature_26) and Facebook Page (Pure Nature Photography). This application is not a public-facing product and does not serve third-party users.

2. Information We Access

To operate this application, we access the following through Meta's APIs:

  • Instagram Business Account information (account ID, username)
  • Facebook Page information (Page ID, Page name)
  • Publishing permissions to post photos and captions on behalf of the account owner
  • Post performance insights (likes, reach, engagement) for analytics purposes

3. How We Use This Information

The information accessed through Meta's APIs is used exclusively to:

  • Publish nature photography posts to Instagram and Facebook automatically
  • Schedule posts at optimal times for engagement
  • Analyze monthly post performance to improve future content
  • Log published posts in a private Excel spreadsheet for personal record-keeping

4. Data Storage

All data processed by Otto-mated is stored locally on the operator's personal computer and in private cloud storage (iCloud Drive). We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-powered photo selection and caption generation
  • Cloudinary — for temporary image hosting required by Meta's API
  • Meta Graph API — for publishing content to Instagram and Facebook

No personal data from Instagram followers or Facebook Page visitors is collected, stored, or processed by this application.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the application (i.e., sending images to Cloudinary for temporary hosting and publishing via Meta's API). No user data from followers or visitors is ever shared.

6. Third-Party Services

Otto-mated integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy

7. Data Retention

Images uploaded to Cloudinary are temporary and automatically deleted after use. No long-term storage of personal data occurs outside of the operator's private local storage.

8. Your Rights

As this application operates solely on behalf of one individual (the operator), no third-party user rights apply. If you have questions about how your data is handled as a follower of @pure_nature_26, please refer to Meta's Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
